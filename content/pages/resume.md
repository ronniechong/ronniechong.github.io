## EXPERIENCE

### Software Engineering Manager / Principal Software Engineer
OpenTable

Spent 9+ years at OpenTable, starting as a pioneer engineer on Business Insights — building the interactive reporting dashboards and D3 data visualisations that helped restaurants make revenue and performance decisions.

Went on to build Private Dining from scratch, scaling it from a pioneer team of 4 to three cross-functional teams (15–18 engineers) in 18 months, driving restaurant adoption up 250%+, and growing Australian engineering from 10 to 25+ (personally running 90% of hiring). Shipped four net-new products along the way, including a Lead Management System processing thousands of leads across 7K+ venues and an AI-powered Self-Serve Onboarding tool that cut activation from days to instant. Also built the team's data lake architecture (Snowflake, Databricks, Parquet), stood up the SRE function, and contributed core components to OpenTable's Design System.

Now the org's sole technical authority on Private Dining — product, design, and cross-functional teams lean on this expertise for architecture decisions and delivery — while owning strategic direction across Business Intelligence, Private Dining, and Operational verticals and managing reliability for platform services consumed by multiple teams.

Also built an internal AI skill plugin marketplace (Grafana, Confluence, Glean, and Span workflows) that engineers now organically pull from for dashboard automation, documentation, and note-taking — establishing AI-assisted practices as a cultural baseline rather than a novelty.
